Street Tacos

Description

Traditional Mexican tacos are called street tacos.
They are typically served on corn tortillas and are stuffed with meat.
Traditional toppings include onions, cilantro, and salsa.
There is no lettuce, tomatoes, or cheese in traditional street tacos.

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Limes
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Ground cumin
  • 2 slices habanero pepper (Optional)
  • Ancho chili powder
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Diced onions
  • Avocados

Directions

  1. Make the chicken taco marinade by stirring together lime juice, olive oil, garlic, cumin chili powder, cilantro, salt & pepper
  2. our marinade over chicken in a bag. Let marinate in fridge 1-6 hours.
  3. Cook on hot preheated grill, about 5 minutes per side.
  4. Let rest briefly, then dice into cubes.
  5. Layer into to warmed tortillas and top as desired.

Tips

  • Be sure to allow at least one hour for marinating otherwise the
    chicken won't have enough time to absorb flavor.
  • Use hot heat to grill and let them char a bit. It gives them a crispy edge
    and adds great flavor!
  • For best results, stick with thighs here. The thighs are juicier, have better
    flavor and brown better. People who don't normally like chicken thighs love
    this recipe. I make them for guests all the time.
